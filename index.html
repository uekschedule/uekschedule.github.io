UEKSchedule

Your schedule manager for Uniwersytet Ekonomiczny w Krakowie

About the App

UEKSchedule is an indispensable app for students of the Cracow University of Economics. It offers convenient tracking of class schedules by allowing the selection of groups and browsing events.

At your disposal:

  • Widgets
  • Teams and Moodle
  • Rector's hours
  • Notifications
  • Class notes
  • Hiding classes
  • Campus map
  • Exporting classes to the system calendar
